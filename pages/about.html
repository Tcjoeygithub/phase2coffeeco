About Phase 2 Coffee Co

The Original Coffee Cart of Kansas City

Our Story

Phase 2 Coffee Co is Kansas City's premier traveling coffee cart, bringing exceptional coffee experiences directly to your special events. Whether it's a wedding, corporate gathering, or private celebration, our mobile coffee cart delivers premium service anywhere in the Kansas City metro area.

The concept of a mobile coffee cart in Kansas City was born from the vision of founders Christian J Lynch and Noah Mills. They wanted to create a coffee business that could travel freely without the overhead costs of a traditional brick-and-mortar shop. While Kansas City has seen coffee trailers and carts before, most emerged as extensions of established coffee shops. Phase 2 Coffee Co changed the game by launching as a dedicated mobile coffee cart service from day one.

We've proudly established ourselves as "The Coffee Cart of Kansas City," bringing specialty coffee catering to events throughout the region. The name "Phase 2" represents our founders' willingness to take risks and step into the next "phase" of their lives, hoping to inspire others to pursue their dreams as well. With the help of our local KC wholesale partner, Marcell Roasters, we're committed to serving the best-tasting coffee in the greater Kansas City area.
