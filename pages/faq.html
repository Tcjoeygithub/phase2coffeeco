Help Center

Find answers to common questions about our mobile coffee cart services in Kansas City. If you don't see your question answered here, please contact us directly.

What are the payment options?

We accept card/bank transfer/check payments for all events and products. If paying with card, there will be a 3.5% transaction fee to cover our software.

What am I paying for when I purchase an event?

Great question! You are paying for all coffee/non-coffee products, customized and homemade syrups, drive expenses, prep and set up, barista fees, and the Phase 2 experience. :)

Do I have to pay a down payment to secure my booked event? And if we cancel after paying, do we get a refund?

YES! 100% of the event cost MUST be paid to officially have your event reserved. We do this to know that you are serious about your event so we can take the right measures to prepare for your event.

There are no refunds after you pay and book with us because that means we are turning people down that possibly wanted your event day and we already purchase all the expenses for your event as well as hired the team to be there that day.

What type of events do you do?

We do any type of event you can think of! From Weddings to Business Events to Graduations to Bridal Showers or even if you wanted a private coffee cart by the poolside in the summer, we are there.

We do offer contracts for corporate companies/medical offices who would like us to be there on a regular basis. Feel free to message us if that pertains to you.
